Doctor Strange

"Doctor Strange" is a mind-bending and visually stunning superhero film that delves into the mystical realms of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Directed by Scott Derrickson and released in 2016, the movie follows the journey of Dr. Stephen Strange, a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon. After a devastating car accident leaves him with severe injuries, Strange seeks unconventional healing in Nepal, where he encounters the enigmatic Ancient One. Under her tutelage, he discovers the mystical arts and learns to harness reality-bending powers. As a new Sorcerer Supreme, Strange must defend the Earth from dark forces, including the sinister sorcerer Kaecilius, who seeks to unleash ancient evil upon the world. Through mind-bending visuals and mind-expanding concepts, "Doctor Strange" takes audiences on a captivating voyage of self-discovery, enlightenment, and the limitless possibilities of the multiverse, solidifying the character as a crucial addition to the ever-expanding Marvel universe.
